Our Story

About Luku Care

Born in Nakuru. Built on care, craft, and community.

A Barbershop With Purpose

Luku Care Hub started with a simple belief: every man deserves to feel confident, respected, and well-groomed — without the hassle of complicated booking.

From our first chair in Rafiki Area to becoming a trusted name across Nakuru, we've stayed true to premium service, fair pricing, and the warmth that defines Kenyan hospitality.

Today, you can walk in, book online, or message us on WhatsApp and Facebook — and get a response within minutes. That's the Luku Care promise.
